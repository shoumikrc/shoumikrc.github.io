  About Me
    I am a  PhD student at the department of Computer and Information Science, Temple University. I am a Research Assistant at DABI, Temple University. My advisor is Professor Zoran Obradovic. My research interest are predictive modelling and statistical analysis of spatio and temporal data. I am also interested in various topics of Bioinformatics, Social network analysis and machine learning. Earlier,  I have completed my MS from the department of ECE at Temple University in 2011. During my Masters' thesis work, my area of research was pattern recognition and moving object tracking in thermal infrared image sequences (link to MS Page).
